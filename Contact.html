Frequently Asked Questions

What should I include in my graphic design portfolio?
In your graphic design portfolio, showcase a variety of your best work, including logos, branding projects, website designs, brochures, posters, and other design pieces. Highlight your strengths and versatility as a designer.
How many pieces should my portfolio consist of?
It's ideal to have around 10 to 15 pieces in your portfolio. Quality is more important than quantity. Select your best work that demonstrates your skills and style.
Should I include personal projects in my portfolio?
Yes, personal projects can showcase your creativity and passion. They can also fill gaps in your portfolio when you're starting out as a designer.
How can I present my portfolio online?
You can create a professional website or use platforms like Behance, Dribbble, or Adobe Portfolio to display your work. Make sure your online portfolio is well-organized and easy to navigate.
What's the best way to get feedback on my portfolio?
Seek feedback from fellow designers, mentors, or online design communities. Honest critiques can help you improve your portfolio and identify areas for growth.
